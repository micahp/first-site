Spitfire

An app that helps you rap

OVERVIEW

Spitfire is a hackathon project I worked on with three friends at HackTX 2015 and won 1st place. I enjoy freestyling and would ask my friends to join in while I drove, but their excuse would always be, "I don't know how to rhyme." Thus, Spitfire! Lowering the barrier to entry for freestyling.
